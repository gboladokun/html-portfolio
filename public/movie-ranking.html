The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Uprising: Wives on Strike 3

Directed by Omoni Oboli, this film is the third installment in the 'Wives on Strike' series. It tells the compelling story of Ebiere, a meat seller whose son is kidnapped, prompting her to embark on a perilous mission to rescue him. The film was released in Nigerian cinemas on October 18, 2024, and became the highest-performing film at the West African Box Office, earning over ₦28 million in its opening weekend. Critics praised its blend of humor and a ctivism, highlighting women's roles as agents of societal change.

The Weekend

Directed by Daniel Oriahi, 'The Weekend' follows Nikiya, an orphan eager to meet her fiancé Luke's family, only to uncover unsettling truths. The film premiered at the Tribeca Festival in June 2024, marking a significant milestone as the first Nigerian film to do so. Upon its Nigerian release on August 30, 2024, it grossed over ₦18 million, reflecting its positive reception. The film received 16 nominations at the 2024 Africa Movie Academy Awards, setting a new record for Nollywood, and won several awards, including Best Film and Best Achievement in Screenplay.

Jagun Jagun

This epic film delves into themes of power, betrayal, and tradition within a Yoruba warrior community. Its rich storytelling and cultural depiction resonated with audiences, earning it critical acclaim. 'Jagun Jagun' received multiple nominations at the 2024 Africa Movie Academy Awards, including Best Achievement in Visual Effects and Best Achievement in Costume Design, showcasing its technical excellence and contribution to Nollywood's evolving landscape.
